This Privacy Policy outlines how Abhishek Singh Shekhawat ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when you use this website (abhisheksinghshekhawat.com) and its related services. I am committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

We may collect the following types of personal information when you voluntarily submit it through forms on this website:

  • Contact Information: Including but not limited to your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Professional Information: Including but not limited to your current role (e.g., Student, Professional), college name, branch/stream, and links to professional profiles (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub).

  • Application-Specific Information: Information you provide regarding your skills, availability, and motivation for participating in collaborative initiatives.

Article 2: How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following internal purposes:

  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries submitted via the contact form and to communicate with you regarding your application for collaborative initiatives.

  • Access Management: To create and manage accounts for secure access to private areas of this website, such as the Gallery.
  • Improving Services: To understand the demographic and professional background of our audience and collaborators, which helps in tailoring opportunities and content.

Article 3: Data Security

I am committed to ensuring that your information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, your data is stored on trusted, secure platforms (e.g., Google Workspace) that utilize industry-standard security measures. This website uses HTTPS (SSL) to encrypt data transmitted between your browser and the server.

Article 4: Data Sharing & Third Parties

Your personal information will not be sold, distributed, or leased to third parties. It is used exclusively for the purposes outlined in Article 2 of this policy.

Article 5: Cookies and Analytics

This website may use cookies to enhance user experience. A cookie is a small file which asks permission to be placed on your computer's hard drive. In the future, we may use traffic log cookies (e.g., via Google Analytics) to identify which pages are being used. This helps us analyze data about webpage traffic and improve our website in order to tailor it to visitor needs. We only use this information for statistical analysis purposes. A cookie in no way gives us access to your computer or any information about you, other than the data you choose to share with us.

Article 6: Your Control Over Your Information

You have the right to control your personal information. You may request details of the personal information which we hold about you. If you believe that any information we are holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, or if you wish for your data to be deleted from our records, please contact me directly at the email address provided below.

Our website may contain links to other websites of interest (e.g., GitHub, LinkedIn, project links). Article 8: Changes to This Policy
