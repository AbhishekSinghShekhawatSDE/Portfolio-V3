Welcome to the collaborative initiative led by Abhishek Singh Shekhawat. These terms are designed to foster a fair, productive, and transparent environment for all participants.

Article 1: Nature of the Initiative

1.1. Purpose: This initiative is primarily an educational, portfolio-building, and community-driven endeavor. Its purpose is to provide a platform for individuals to gain real-world experience, collaborate on tangible projects, and enhance their skills in a practical setting.

1.2. No Employment Relationship: Participation in this initiative, in any capacity, does not constitute or create an employer-employee relationship, a partnership, or a joint venture between you and Abhishek Singh Shekhawat. Contributors are independent participants and are not entitled to any benefits associated with formal employment.

Article 2: Roles & Responsibilities

2.1. Role Definitions: Roles within the initiative are defined based on project needs. You will be assigned to a role (e.g., Technical, Creative, Management) based on your application and skill set.

2.2. Contributor's Responsibilities: You are expected to contribute actively and consistently to the best of your ability, communicate professionally with team members, adhere to project timelines and guidelines, and uphold the Code of Conduct (Article 5).

Article 3: Compensation & Rewards

3.1. Voluntary Contribution: It is expressly understood that the majority of contributor roles are voluntary and uncompensated. The primary reward is the experience, portfolio material, mentorship, and networking opportunities gained.

3.2. Performance-Based Rewards: Specific, high-impact or leadership roles may be eligible for performance-based stipends, bonuses, or other rewards. Any such arrangement is not guaranteed and will be determined at the sole discretion of the project lead. If a role is eligible for compensation, it will be communicated and agreed upon via a separate, formal written offer before the commencement of work.

3.3. Certificates: Verifiable Certificates of Contribution will be issued based on the successful completion of assigned tasks and a demonstrated commitment to the project, as determined by the project lead.

Article 4: Intellectual Property (IP)

4.1. 4.2. Attribution: While the project owns the IP, you will receive full and prominent attribution for your specific Contributions. This credit will be formally recognized in project documentation, your portfolio, and any relevant public showcases, ensuring you can leverage this experience for your professional career.

4.3. Pre-existing Work: You warrant that your Contributions are your own original work and do not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party.

Article 5: Code of Conduct

5.1. Professionalism: All participants are required to maintain a high standard of professionalism in all communications and interactions.

5.2. Respect: A respectful and inclusive environment is mandatory. Harassment, discrimination, or any form of abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

5.3. Collaboration: This is a team effort. A positive, collaborative mindset and a willingness to both give and receive constructive feedback are essential.

Article 6: Confidentiality

During your participation, you may be exposed to proprietary ideas, strategies, or early-stage project details. You agree to maintain the confidentiality of such information and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

Article 7: Termination of Participation

7.1. By Contributor: You may choose to cease your contribution at any time by providing formal notice to the project lead.

7.2. By Project Lead: The project lead reserves the right to terminate your participation in the initiative at any time for reasons including, but not limited to, a breach of these terms, violation of the Code of Conduct, or prolonged inactivity.
