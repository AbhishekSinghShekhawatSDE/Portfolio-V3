My Story

Hello! I'm Abhishek Singh Shekhawat, a driven Computer Science Engineering student at Poornima University, Jaipur (2022–2026), with a strong inclination toward DevOps, Cloud Computing, and automation-driven solutions. My core focus lies in building scalable infrastructure, streamlining development pipelines, and leveraging modern cloud-native architectures.

I’ve cultivated hands-on experience through projects and internships where I used tools like GitHub, CI/CD pipelines, basic scripting with Python, and version control systems. I'm actively exploring platforms like AWS, containerization, and infrastructure-as-code.

Outside the terminal, I bring in a background in digital marketing, video editing, and community leadership, having served as a campus ambassador and earned the prestigious Rashtrapati Scout Award — a testament to my discipline and team ethic.

I'm driven by curiosity, collaborative energy, and a passion for solving real-world problems. Currently, I’m looking for opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and grow alongside high-performing tech teams.
